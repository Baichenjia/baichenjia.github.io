Chenjia Bai

Hi, this is Chenjia Bai (白辰甲). I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), advised by Prof. Peng Liu. Currently, I am a visiting student at University of Toronto , working with Prof. Animesh Garg . My research focuses on Reinforcement Learning (RL), including offline RL, efficient exploration, risk-sensitive RL, and multi-goal RL.

Before that, I was a visiting student at Northwestern University (remotely), working with Prof. Zhaoran Wang . I also used to be an intern at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab (advised by Prof. Jianye Hao), Tencent Robotics X (advised by Dr. Lei han), and Alibaba. I received my Bachelor's degree and Master's degree in Computer Science from HIT.
